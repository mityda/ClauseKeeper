FAQ

Why pay if it’s open source?

Why pay if I can self-host for free?

You are not paying for secret code. You are paying for maintenance, legal-change monitoring, automatic updates, hosting and versioned URLs so your documents stay current without ops work.

Is the hosted version different from the open-source one?

It uses the same core engine. Cloud adds managed updates, hosting, billing and the operational pieces needed to keep live documents current.

Can I start self-hosted and move to Cloud later?

Yes. Start self-hosted if that is right for your team, then move to Cloud when you want ClauseKeeper maintained and hosted for you.

Will Cloud lock me in?

No. The AGPL code stays available, and your documents remain yours. Cloud is the convenience layer: we do the watching so you do not have to.
